HYMN TO ST. ALOYSIUS

The youth who wealth and courts despised
Thine spotless mind above to raise;
who every rising thought chastised
'Tis Aloysius claims our ways.

Chorus: O gentle and angelic youth
Aloysius pray for us
O gentle and angelic youth, pray for us,
Aloysius pray for us.

Angelic youth, O glorious saint,
In cradle God's name ever invoked:
In life his protection provoked
His blessing thou didst share.

The tenor of your life so bright,
So pure as Angle's purity;
A seraph from the realms of light
Dwelling on earth you seemed to be.
